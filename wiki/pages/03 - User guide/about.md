## About

This dialog is shown by selecting the option from the menu.

The version numbers are read by querying your installed software, they are not haard-coded in the dialog.

To find more information about Gramps, please visit the Gramps Project Web page:

- [`https://gramps-project.org`](https://gramps-project.org)

### Original author

Gramps was written by Donald N. Allingham (don@gramps-project.org).
