![[_media/Gramp-Small-background-wiki-2020.jpg]] **Research, organize and share your family tree with Gramps.**

Gramps is a free software project and community. We strive to produce a genealogy program that is both intuitive for hobbyists and feature-complete for professional genealogists. It is a community project, created, developed and governed by genealogists.

## Abstract

This **Gramps [Wiki](wiki:Gramps_Glossary#wiki) Manual** for version 6.0 is the online user's guide, detailing most aspects of the Gramps application, a database for the collection of genealogical information. As of , , the [most current Gramps release](wiki:Download) is the version. Both Gramps and the accompanying Wiki Manual [have been translated into multiple languages](wiki:User_manual_translations).

You can also download the **Complete 6.0 Offline Manual** ([English 20 MiB PDF](wiki::Media:Gramps6.0UserManual.pdf) and [additional languages](wiki:User_manual_translations#Gramps_6.0.x_Manual)) last updated 2025-07-17. For previous Wiki Manuals revisions, see the [archival versions](wiki:User_manual_translations#superseded) made for [previous releases of Gramps](wiki:Previous_releases_of_Gramps). (Search the page for "*offline PDF*" to find offline versions.) Volunteer WikiContributors started the 6.0 addition drafts to the 5.2 base pages on 23 March 2025. Users are urged to explore new features and help add documentation in the wiki.

The **6.0 Addons Offline Manual** is available for review at ([English 18 MiB PDF](wiki:Media:GrampsAddonsUserManual6.0.pdf)) last updated 2025-07-17.

To explore other sources of information about using Gramps, see the [Information Portal](wiki::Portal:Using_Gramps).
